What Is Arduino???

The Arduino is a prototyping platform that performs the transmission and decoding of programming signals. Widely used in robotics, mechanics and electronics projects, the arduino works as a kind of brain for processing information that coordinates electronic circuits.


Equipped with input and output connections for both computers and electronic hardware, the arduino works by mediating information between these devices. Thus, the arduino receives external signals, sends these signals to its processor and, according to the operations for which it is programmed, performs subsequent operations.
